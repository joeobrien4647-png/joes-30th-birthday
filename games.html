▾
How to Play

Try Not to Laugh

Sit in the hot seat for 60 seconds. Everyone tries to make you crack. No touching allowed.

+3 pts Individual ⏱ 30m
Pending
▾
How to Play
  1. One person sits in "the hot seat" (a chair facing everyone)
  2. They must keep a completely straight face for 60 seconds
  3. Everyone else does ANYTHING to make them laugh - jokes, impressions, weird noises, ridiculous dances
  4. Rules: No touching the person in the seat!
  5. If they laugh, smile, or crack in ANY way - they're OUT
  6. Each team nominates 2 people. Knockout rounds until a champion is crowned
Scoring
  • Last person standing from each team: +3 pts for their team
  • Overall champion (longest without cracking): +2 individual bonus
📷 Film This: Close-ups of the face slowly cracking. The micro-expressions are universally hilarious.

Pétanque Tournament

Classic French boules - team knockout rounds on the chateau grounds

+5 pts Team Game ⏱ 1h 🎯 Pétanque set
Pending
▾
How to Play
  1. 5 teams play a knockout bracket (2 semis, losers playoff, final)
  2. Each match: 3 players per team, 2 boules each
  3. Throw the cochonnet (jack), then take turns throwing boules
  4. Closest boule to the jack scores. First to 11 points wins the match
  5. You CAN knock opponents' boules away - this is France, we play hard
Scoring
  • Tournament winner: +5 pts
  • Runner up: +3 pts
  • Best individual shot (crowd vote): +1 bonus
📷 Film This: The tension of a close measure and the celebrations when a clutch shot lands.

Charades Relay: Telephone Edition

Chinese whispers meets charades. Act it out down the line - watch it degrade hilariously.

+5 pts Team Game ⏱ 30m 🎭 Clue cards
Pending
▾
How to Play
  1. Teams line up single-file, all facing the same direction
  2. Last person in line sees a scenario on a card (no one else looks!)
  3. They tap the person in front, who turns around
  4. Act out the clue to ONLY that person - no sounds!
  5. That person then acts out what they THINK they saw to the next person
  6. Continue down the line until the first person guesses the original clue
Example Clues
  • "Joe eating a baguette wearing a beret"
  • "Sophie trying to parallel park"
  • "A French waiter who hates British tourists"
  • "Someone discovering a spider in their room"
Scoring
  • Correct guess: +5 pts
  • Close guess (judge's discretion): +2 pts
  • Funniest mutation (crowd vote): +2 bonus
📷 Film This: Film the original action AND the final guess side by side. The comparison is always comedy gold.
