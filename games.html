ğŸ² Dice Game

Roll the dice, follow the rules:

  • 1: Waterfall - everyone drinks until the person before stops
  • 2: You - pick someone to drink
  • 3: Me - you drink
  • 4: Floor - last to touch the floor drinks
  • 5: Guys drink
  • 6: Girls drink
