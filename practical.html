Group Expenses

Shares 1 day = 1 share per person
  • How it works: Joe will handle all big group purchases (groceries, communal drinks, BBQ supplies, etc.) and track the total spend
  • Fair split: 1 day at the chateau = 1 share. If you're there 5 days you get 5 shares, 3 days = 3 shares, etc.
  • What you owe: Your shares ÷ total shares × total spend. So you only pay for the days you're actually there
  • Not included: Meals out, personal bar tabs, activity costs, or anything bought between smaller groups — sort those between yourselves
  • Settlement: Joe will send everyone their total after the trip
  • Estimated cost: ~£10–15 per person per night (food, drink & supplies). For 5 nights that's roughly £50–75 total
